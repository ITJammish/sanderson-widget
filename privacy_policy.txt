Sanderson Widget Privacy Policy

-Data-
This app neither collects, stores nor shares any data. This includes user (personal, behavioural or otherwise) or device data.

-Ads-
This app contains no advertisements.

-App access-
This app contains no restricted features. All features are freely available, and do not require an account/registration of any kind.

-Target audience and content-
This app contains appropriate content for any user group. The app allows the user to open blog articles from https://www.brandonsanderson.com/, posted by the Author Brandon Sanderson and his team.
